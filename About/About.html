About Us

Our Story

R.A.K showcases the exceptional talents of the famed Kimsan. This pioneering chef has created an entirely new category for authentic Khmer fine dining which she first started at a Siem Reap restaurant called “Embassy”. She has brought their culinary skills to Phnom Penh at Sombok Restaurant.

Meet the Chef

Mr.Nak is a respective graduate of the well-known Paul Dubrule and Salabai hospitality schools. The local legend in the culinary scene she has skillfully risen to the top of an industry typically dominated by males. Her passion for cooking and extensive experience has taken her all over the world, but her heart still remains with Cambodia and its unique flavours.

About our Group

As a proud member of the Restaurant Collection, our goal is straightforward: to offer you an unparalleled dining experience tailored to your tastes. In our establishments, your culinary preferences always take center stage. Embassy Restaurant invites you on a culinary odyssey where flavors converge to create a symphony for your taste buds. Our menu is a celebration of Khmer cuisines, providing a passport to exquisite fine dining experiences in Siem Reap. Join us and relish the artistry of culinary exploration.
